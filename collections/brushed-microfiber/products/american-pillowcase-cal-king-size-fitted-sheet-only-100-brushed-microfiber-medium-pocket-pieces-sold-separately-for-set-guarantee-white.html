Add some luxurious comfort to your bedroom with American Pillowcase Microfiber Bed Sheets. These sheets are made of 100% double brushed Microfiber for that silky smooth, peach-fuzz feel. Comparable to 5-star hotel materials, these breathable sheets are hypoallergenic and even prevent dust mite issues. The stylish sheets are great for any season; they'll keep you cool on hot summer nights, and warm in the darkest of winters. The durable linens are long-lasting and will maintain their HIGH-QUALITY wash after wash; keeping their stain-, fade-, and wrinkle-resistant qualities.



Includes: One Microfiber Fitted Sheet with Medium 12 Inch Pockets



Microfiber Fitted Sheets



These fitted sheets have been pre-shrunk, with 12-inch pockets deep enough to easily install over your mattress without worry of it slipping up. To help ensure the fitted sheets stay put, elastic is installed all the way around the edges of the sheets, rather than just in the corners. Thanks to the expert finishing, the fabric's silky softness and smoothness is the perfect addition to your bedroom decor.



Durable Quality; Built Tough



These durable sheets are long-lasting and affordable. Their lightweight, yet long lasting material is perfect for those with allergies. These breathable sheets are hypoallergenic and even prevent dust mite issues. The microfiber material helps reduce allergy reaction and respiratory irritation. When paired with regular washings, these sheets are sure to meet those hypoallergenic needs.



Wrinkle- and Stain-Resistant



This material is processed in a way that makes this durable fabric maintain their overall brightness and shape for longer periods of time. Additionally, this helps the linens stay wrinkle free and let you enjoy a smooth finish.



The tight weave of these microfibers prevents liquids from soaking in immediately, helping keep these fabrics stain resistant. Quickly clean up your spill before it has a chance to soak in and your sheet will remain stain free.



Care Instructions



When it is time to wash your pre-shrunk sheets, simply toss them into an ordinary washing machine with hot or warm water with mild detergent.  Washing at a warmer temperature allows the microfibers to expand, releasing trapped dirt and micro particles.



Air dry is best for these products since microfiber dries relatively quickly.  If you do need to use a dryer, set it on low or no heat.
