American Pillowcase has been selling Turkish cotton bath towels for more than 15 years and contracts with only the very best mills in Turkey. Presently American Pillowcase has 5 of the top selling pure cotton bath towels on Amazon. This can't be done without continually providing superb products at great prices. Bath towels made in Turkey are softer and more durable because it is the only country which does not use any bleach in the manufacturing process, including our hotel collection white towels. SALBAKOS luxury cotton towels are great to take to the gym for a workout. You will immediately recognize the quality, much like that of hair towels found at a high end salon. All Turkish bath towels are vat-dyed which means the dye is infused into the yarns under high pressure and high temperature for 6-12 hours depending upon the color. This limits any fading of even the brightest colors. Even our pure white bath towels are dyed white, not bleach white. For maximum color retention and softness, do not use any bleach when washing these Turkish cotton towels. You will feel the softness immediately, and these towels will continually get softer with each washing. And thanks to using only the finest Turkish cotton and double-stitching all the hems, the durability is unequaled. These bath towels should last you many years! In addition, these luxury spa-quality bath towels are made in an Oeko-Tex and ISO 9001 mill. This means that all materials must be chemical-free and pesticide-free and that the mill operates at the highest quality standards and is audited regularly.
